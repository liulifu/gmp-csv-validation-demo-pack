## 1. Request

**System:** Legacy LIMS 6.2  
**Servers:** LIMS-APP-01, LIMS-DB-01 (Oracle 12c), legacy interface services  
**Requested retirement date:** 2028-03-31  
**Requester:** Legacy System Owner

## 2. Business justification

QCLabOne 2.0 has operated in a validated state since 2027-06-15. Active records and processes have migrated; all legacy records are archived in ArchiveVault; retrieval evidence is accepted. Continuing Legacy LIMS increases unsupported software, security, licence and duplicate-system risk.
