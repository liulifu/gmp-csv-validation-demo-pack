Legacy LIMS 6.2 was retired on 2028-03-31 after successful QCLabOne operation, complete archive transfer, approved retrievability evidence and controlled shutdown.

## 3. Official record statement

From 2028-03-31, ArchiveVault is the official source for Legacy LIMS records. QCLabOne is the official source for records created in QCLabOne and for migrated active records according to the migration mapping. The sealed legacy system image is contingency-only and not for routine use.

## 4. Retirement decision

Retirement is complete and approved. The system shall not be returned to routine service. Any recovery from the sealed image requires QA/Records approval and a documented incident/change.
