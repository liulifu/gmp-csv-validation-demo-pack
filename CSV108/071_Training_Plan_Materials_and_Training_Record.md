## 1. Training objective

Ensure each user has the knowledge and demonstrated ability required for assigned system activities before production access.

## 2. Curriculum and completion

| Role | Curriculum | Method | Completion by 2027-06-10 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Analyst | 66 User Operation SOP; data integrity; LES; direct/manual entry; e-signature; OOS/outage | Instructor-led + hands-on assessment | 42/42 complete |

## 3. Assessment criteria

Knowledge assessment pass mark is 80%. Hands-on assessment requires successful completion of a role-relevant scenario without critical error. A failed assessment requires retraining and reassessment; access is not granted until passed.

## 4. Training materials

TRN-M01 system/intended use; M02 sample/LES; M03 data integrity and corrections; M04 review/audit trail; M05 OOS/OOT; M06 e-signature; M07 contingency; M08 administration/security; M09 backup/incident/change; M10 archive/retrieval.

## 5. Access linkage

The access request references the completed training curriculum and role. Training expiry, role change or major system/process change triggers reassessment.

## 6. Record conclusion

All 97 planned participants completed required training before go-live. No production account was enabled solely on attendance without required assessment.
